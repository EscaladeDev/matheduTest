Outcomes

Weekly learning goals

  1. Classify, operate with, and simplify expressions involving real numbers with full justification.
  2. Apply all exponent rules, including negative and zero exponents, fluently.
  3. Convert between standard and scientific notation and use it in context.
  4. Explain the why behind each property and rule.
  5. Self-assess common pitfalls and track error patterns.
